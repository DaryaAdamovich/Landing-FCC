Max Korzh

Simple words, but a whole gamut of feelings and thoughts..

Max Korzh is a young singer from Belarus who easily mixes electronic music, rap and soulful singing in his work. The story of Max's takeoff is incredibly rapid and happening right before our eyes. In 2012, he released his first solo album, and year later he got thousands of fans at his concerts. In 2018, Max made a big tour of Russia, and his hits entered the top-10 most listened to in Apple Music!

2013 - Live Dope/ Life Is Good
